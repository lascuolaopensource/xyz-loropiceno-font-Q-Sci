# 🔤 TUTU

TUTU was collectively designed by a team of designers and non-designers during the **["Viaggio Intorno al Sole"](https://viaggiointornoalsole.lascuolaopensource.xyz)** XYZ workshop by **[La Scuola Open Source](https://lascuolaopensource.xyz)** in Tuturano (Brindisi, Italy), from February 20–28, 2026, and released under the **SIL Open Font License 1.1** in 2026.

TUTU is an **experimental display typeface** and a living project, designed to evolve and expand with new glyphs over time. The typeface draws deep inspiration from **uncial scripts** and **medieval calligraphic traditions**, reflecting the heritage of local medieval festivals. It bridges the gap between historical legacy and contemporary experimental design, performing with a distinct character at both large and smaller scales. In addition there is also a font with icons/pictograms full of reference to the local culture and traditions.

Letterforms were designed using **[Chirone](https://github.com/sssuperio/chirone)**, a tool forked from [GTL](https://github.com/bbtgnn/GTL-web) and developed during the workshop.
